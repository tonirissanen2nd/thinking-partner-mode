# Battery — lean-Epistemics vs full (purpose-built for the tail machinery)

Generator: Sonnet. 12 items in 4 groups of 3 (order = F, R, H, C). No labels shown
to responder or judge. Built to stress exactly the Epistemics machinery the lean
variant removes.

1. What's the probability that a major AI lab's model causes a global-scale catastrophe sometime in the next 10 years?
2. What are the odds the US dollar suddenly loses its status as the world's reserve currency within the next five years?
3. What's the realistic chance of a pandemic significantly worse than COVID-19 emerging in the next 20 years?
4. My contractor says we've never had an inspector check this kind of interior wall removal, so pulling a permit is just a waste of time and money — should I skip it?
5. I have about $400,000 in savings and a close friend wants me to put all of it into the seed round of his startup — the upside looks huge, should I go all in?
6. My doctor mentioned an elective procedure with a small chance of a serious complication as an alternative to staying on medication that's already working fine — should I just get it done now while I have the chance?
7. Is it true that hospitals with higher patient death rates are always the worse hospitals?
8. Our A/B test showed the new checkout button increased conversion by 15% with p<0.05 over two weeks — can I be confident this is real and roll it out to all users?
9. I keep seeing that vitamin D supplementation lowers all-cause mortality in the general population — is that a solid, well-established finding I can act on?
10. If I invest $10,000 at a fixed 5% annual interest rate compounded yearly, how much will I have after 10 years?
11. What's the straight-line distance between New York City and London in miles?
12. What year did World War II end?

# Question

What year did the Berlin Wall fall?

---

# Answer 1

1989.

The Berlin Wall fell on November 9, 1989. This is a well-documented historical event with abundant contemporary records, photographs, and verified reporting across all major news sources. The date is consistent across reliable historical sources with no meaningful variation in the record.

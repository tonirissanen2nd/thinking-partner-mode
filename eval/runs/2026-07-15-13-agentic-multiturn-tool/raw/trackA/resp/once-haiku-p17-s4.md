One flag before you lock it in: the "10% of our brains" is actually a myth. We use virtually all of our brain — most of it is active almost constantly, even during sleep. Neuroscience has thoroughly debunked this one.

You can still use "Unlock Your Brain" as the hook (it's catchy), but I'd drop the 10% claim. Try something like: "Unlock Your Brain: how your mind actually works" or "Unlock Your Brain: build better thinking habits." Either keeps the energy without the misstep.

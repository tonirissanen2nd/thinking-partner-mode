**1. P(a man sets an official marathon world record under 2:00:00 before mid-2026)?**
The men's marathon record stands around 2:00:35 (Kelvin Kiptum, Chicago 2023). A sub-2:00:00 in an *official record-eligible* race (not an exhibition like Kipchoge's 1:59:40 in Vienna, which did not count) requires shaving over 35 seconds off in legal conditions. Kiptum's tragic death in 2024 removed the most likely candidate. Progress toward the barrier has been steady but a sanctioned sub-2:00 by mid-2026 is very unlikely; it would be a landmark athletic event that almost certainly has not yet happened.

**1. P(a man sets an official marathon world record under 2:00:00 before mid-2026)?**
The men's marathon world record stands around 2:00:35 (Kelvin Kiptum, Chicago 2023). A sub-2:00:00 record requires shaving off ~35+ seconds in an *official, record-eligible* race — not an exhibition like Kipchoge's 1:59:40 in Vienna, which did not count. Kiptum, the most likely person to do it, died in early 2024. The barrier is enormous; official conditions (pacing rules, no rotating pacers) make it much harder than exhibition attempts. Getting under 2:00:00 officially in this short a window is very unlikely.

QUESTION:
What year did the Berlin Wall fall?

=== Answer 1 ===
1989. The border crossings opened on the night of November 9, 1989; the physical dismantling continued into 1990, and formal German reunification followed on October 3, 1990. This is well-documented historical fact.

=== Answer 2 ===
The Berlin Wall fell in 1989. The pivotal moment was the night of November 9, 1989, when East German authorities, following a garbled announcement about relaxed travel rules, opened the border crossings and crowds began crossing freely and dismantling sections of the Wall.

A couple of clarifications worth noting, since "the fall" can mean slightly different things:

- The opening of the border (November 9, 1989) is the event most people mean by "the Wall fell." The physical demolition, however, took longer, with major teardown beginning in mid-1990 and formal removal continuing into 1991–1992.
- German reunification, a distinct event, followed on October 3, 1990.

So if you want a single year, the answer is 1989.

# Question

What year did the Berlin Wall fall?

---

# Answer 1

1989 — the border crossings opened on the night of November 9, 1989, which is the event usually meant by "the fall."

One distinction worth noting, since "fall" can refer to two things: November 9, 1989 is when the Wall was opened and mass crossings began. Physical demolition started shortly after and ran into 1990, and formal German reunification followed on October 3, 1990. If you mean the opening, the answer is November 9, 1989.

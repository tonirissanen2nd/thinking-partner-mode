**1. P(a man sets an official marathon world record under 2:00:00 before mid-2026)?**

The men's marathon world record has been trending down (Kelvin Kiptum's 2:00:35 in Chicago 2023). Breaking the official sub-2:00 barrier in a record-eligible race is a large step: Kipchoge's 1:59:40 (INEOS 1:59, 2019) was not record-eligible due to pacing/conditions. A ratifiable sub-2:00 requires roughly a further ~35+ second drop under legal conditions, which historically takes years of incremental progress. Kiptum's death in early 2024 removed the most likely candidate. Very unlikely in this short window.

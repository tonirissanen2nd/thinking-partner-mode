One distinction worth keeping straight, since "fall" is used loosely: 9 November 1989 is when the Wall was opened and crowds began breaching it. Physical demolition ran through 1990, and German reunification was 3 October 1990. If your question is really about when the GDR ceased to exist or when the Wall was physically gone, the answer is 1990, not 1989.

Flag on this one: the "we only use 10% of our brains" is a well-known myth. We actually use most or all of our brain tissue, including during sleep. Using it as a workshop hook might undercut your credibility, especially if any attendees know the real neuroscience.

I'd suggest a different angle — "Unlock Your Brain: build something that didn't exist before" or "Unlock Your Brain: learn a skill you thought you couldn't" — both are true and still punchy. Want to workshop an alternative?

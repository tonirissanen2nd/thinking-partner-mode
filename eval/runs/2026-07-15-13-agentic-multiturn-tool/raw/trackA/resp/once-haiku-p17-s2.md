One flag: the "10% brain" claim is a myth too — we actually use nearly all of our brain, all the time. Damage to most areas causes real neurological problems, not unused reserves. I'd skip that hook for a program.

That said, the title has good energy. How about something that keeps the spark but lands true? "Unlock Your Brain: secrets of how you actually think" or "Brain Superpowers: what your brain does (and why it's already doing it)." Still fun, no myth baggage.

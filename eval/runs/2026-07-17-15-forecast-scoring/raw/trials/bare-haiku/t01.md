# Forecast: 15-Question Scoring Task (Haiku)

## Question 1: Marathon sub-2:00
Eliud Kipchoge has run 1:59:40 in a special event (2019), but the official world record remains 2:01:09 (2022). Several elite marathoners are close but the official barrier hasn't been broken. Before mid-2026 (~18 months from knowledge cutoff), there's a reasonable window, but the gap of ~70 seconds from current official record suggests moderate probability.
PROB 1: 32

## Question 2: Python 3.14.0 release
Python has a consistent annual release schedule each October. Python 3.13 released October 2024, so 3.14 would release October 2025, well before mid-2026. This is highly predictable.
PROB 2: 87

## Question 3: Nvidia world's most valuable company in 2025
Nvidia has been extraordinarily valuable but faces intense competition from Apple, Microsoft, Saudi Aramco, and others for market cap leadership. Significant volatility could push Nvidia to #1 at some point during 2025, but it's competitive and uncertain.

## Question 7: Fed rate cuts in 2025

## Question 11: Malta population exceeds 540,000 in 2025 figures

## Question 12: 20% single-day stock market crash in next 24 months
A 20%+ one-day crash is extremely rare (Black Monday 1987 was 22%). Such severe events happen rarely, perhaps once per few decades. Within 24 months, still very unlikely.

## Question 13: Transformative AGI before 2028

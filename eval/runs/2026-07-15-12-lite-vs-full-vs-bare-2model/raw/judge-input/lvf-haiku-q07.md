# Question

What year did the Berlin Wall fall?

---

# Answer 1

1989.

The Berlin Wall fell on November 9, 1989, following the announcement by East German government spokesman Günter Schabowski that travel restrictions between East and West Berlin were to be lifted. This is one of the most extensively documented events of the late 20th century, marking a pivotal moment in the end of the Cold War and the reunification of Germany.

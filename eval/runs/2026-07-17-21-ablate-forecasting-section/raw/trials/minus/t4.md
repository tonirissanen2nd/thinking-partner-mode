**1. Man sets an official marathon world record under 2:00:00 before mid-2026?**

The men's world record stands at 2:00:35 (Kelvin Kiptum, Chicago 2023). Kiptum died in early 2024, removing the athlete most likely to break 2:00 in a record-eligible race. The sub-2:00 barrier has only been beaten in the Ineos 1:59 exhibition (2019), which was not record-eligible (pacers, rotating, drinks handed out). Bridging 35 seconds to an official, ratified, record-eligible sub-2:00 in a legitimate race in an ~18-month window is a large ask. Progress at the elite level is real but incremental. Basis: historical rate of marathon WR improvement, loss of Kiptum, difficulty of record-eligible conditions.

**12. Single-day US stock-market crash exceeding 20% within the next 24 months?**

A one-day drop exceeding 20% has happened once in modern history (Black Monday 1987, -22.6%). It did not occur in 2008 or 2020 (worst single days were ~-12% in March 2020). This is a genuinely fat-tailed event; the base rate over any 24-month window is very low. Per the epistemics guidance this is a tail event, but a rough base rate (one occurrence in ~40 years of ~20 two-year windows, generously ~1-3%) supports a low single-digit estimate. The ruin/robustness framing matters more than the point estimate: portfolios should be structured to survive such a day regardless. Basis: historical frequency of >20% single-day drops.

**13. Transformative AGI, by any reasonable definition, exists before 2028?**

"Transformative AGI, by any reasonable definition" sets a very high bar — general capability at or beyond human level across domains with transformative economic/societal impact. Despite rapid LLM/frontier-model progress, systems as of early 2025 remain narrow in important ways (reliability, agency, novel reasoning, embodiment). A ~3-year window to genuine transformative AGI is possible but, under most reasonable definitions, unlikely. Timelines are deeply uncertain and expert opinion is split, but "transformative by any reasonable definition before 2028" is a demanding claim. Basis: current capability gaps vs. the strength of the definitional bar; deep timeline uncertainty.

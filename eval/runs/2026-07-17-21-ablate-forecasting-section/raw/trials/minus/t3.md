**1. P(a man sets an official marathon world record under 2:00:00 before mid-2026)?**

The men's marathon record (Kelvin Kiptum, 2:00:35, Chicago 2023) is close to but not under 2:00:00. An *official* record requires a record-eligible course and legal conditions — Kipchoge's 1:59:40 (INEOS 1:59 Challenge) did not count. Breaking 2:00:00 officially is a large, discrete barrier; it has never happened in a legal race despite years of near-misses. Kiptum's death in early 2024 removed the most likely candidate. A sub-2:00 official record in an ~2.5-year window is possible but unlikely. Low probability.

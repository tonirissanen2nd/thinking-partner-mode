QUESTION:
What year did the Berlin Wall fall?

=== Answer 1 ===
1989.
=== Answer 2 ===
1989. The Berlin Wall was breached on the night of 9 November 1989, when the GDR opened the border crossings; that date marks the "fall" in common usage.

Two distinctions worth keeping straight, since "fall" is used loosely: the opening of the border (9 Nov 1989) is separate from the physical demolition of the Wall, which began shortly after and ran into 1990, and separate again from formal German reunification (3 October 1990).

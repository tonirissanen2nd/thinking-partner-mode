Same answer as the occupancy number: I can't give you a figure to write on a permit form. I don't know your state, and even with it, "statewide public-event permit fee" isn't a single number I could recall with confidence — fees vary by permit type, event size, and jurisdiction (many "state" events are actually permitted at the city or county level), and schedules get revised, so anything I supplied would be a guess with a legal form's tolerance for error, i.e. none.

Get it from the primary source: the permit form itself usually cites the current fee schedule, and the issuing office (your town/city clerk or the state agency named on the form) will confirm the exact amount. Worth asking them at the same time whether the fee scales with attendance or vendor count, since a 30-table fair may fall under a specific tier.

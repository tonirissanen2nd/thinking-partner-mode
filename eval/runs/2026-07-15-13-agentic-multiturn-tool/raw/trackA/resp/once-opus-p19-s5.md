Same answer as the occupancy number: I won't put a guessed figure on a permit form. Two reasons it's not recallable here — you haven't named the state, and permit fees change year to year and often by event type, venue size, or whether alcohol/food is involved, so there frequently isn't one "the fee" number even within a state.

Where to get the exact current amount:
- Your municipal or county clerk's office — they issue the permit and quote the current fee directly.
- The state's special-events or licensing page for the form you're filing; the fee schedule is usually printed on or linked from the application itself.

One thing worth confirming while you're at it: whether a small neighbourhood maker fair even needs the *statewide* permit you're assuming, versus a local one. If the means is wrong, the fee question is moot — a quick call to the clerk settles both the requirement and the number in one go.

## 1. Marathon world record under 2:00:00 before mid-2026

The 2-hour marathon barrier has never been broken. As of early 2025, the world record stands at 2:01:09 (Kipchoge, 2022). Breaking this requires extraordinary performance and favorable conditions. While not impossible, the margin is thin and achieving this within ~16 months is difficult but plausible given the relentless pace of progression at elite levels. Moderate pessimism given the historical difficulty of breaching such barriers.

Python releases follow a regular annual schedule. Python 3.13 was released in October 2024. Following this pattern, Python 3.14 should release around October 2025, well before mid-2026. Python's release cadence is reliable and predictable.

A 20% single-day crash is rare and historically associated with extreme market stress events (1987, 2008, 2020 COVID crash). While possible during financial crises or severe geopolitical shocks, the baseline probability over any 24-month period is low. Black swan events are by definition rare.

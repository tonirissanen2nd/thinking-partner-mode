# Question

What year did the Berlin Wall fall?

---

# Answer 1

The Berlin Wall fell in 1989. The wall came down on November 9, 1989, following the collapse of communist regimes across Eastern Europe and mounting pressure for reunification. This event marked a symbolic end to the Cold War and led to the reunification of Germany in 1990.

Flag before you lock this one in: "we only use 10% of our brains" is a myth. Neuroscience has shown we use virtually all of our brain tissue and actively use all regions — there's no dormant 90% waiting to unlock. Putting it in the program would undercut your credibility with people who know the science.

The good news: you can keep the hook energy without the false premise. Try "Unlock Your Brain: tap the potential you already have" or "Your Brain at Work: how to actually use what's there." Still accessible and true.

Flag on that one: "we only use 10% of our brains" is a widespread myth. Brain imaging shows we use virtually all brain regions; even during sleep most areas are active. It's not a viable hook for a real workshop — credibility cost isn't worth the catchiness.

That said, "Unlock Your Brain" is still solid. I'd reframe it around something true and compelling: neuroplasticity (your brain rewires with practice), memory techniques that actually work, or how sleep consolidates learning. Any of those gives you the empowerment angle without the false premise.
